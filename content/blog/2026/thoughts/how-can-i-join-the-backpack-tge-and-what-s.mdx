## TL;DR

Key Takeaway: Backpack's Token Generation Event (TGE) is set for March 23, 2026, unlocking 25% of its 1B total supply (250M tokens) via airdrop: 24% to points program participants and 1% to Mad Lads NFT holders. The verification deadline for eligibility passed at 12:00 AM UTC on March 15, 2026 (16 hours ago as of 2026-03-15 16:14 UTC), closing the window for new users to qualify for this initial distribution. Eligible verified users will receive tokens automatically, with claim details forthcoming closer to TGE. Future unlocks (37.5% pre-IPO) offer potential ongoing participation through platform growth. [Backpack Learn](https://learn.backpack.exchange/articles/backpack-tge)[Coinness](https://coinness.com/en/news/1151652)

Backpack, a Solana-focused exchange and wallet platform, structured its token launch to prioritize users over insiders—no team or VC allocations at TGE. This user-centric approach rewards trading activity via points and NFT holdings, while tying future supply to verifiable milestones like regulatory wins or product launches. With the deadline passed, focus shifts to monitoring claims and post-TGE engagement for additional rewards.
