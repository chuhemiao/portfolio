## TL;DR

Hyperliquid rolled out two significant developments in early March 2026: the HIP-4 testnet activation on March 11 introducing USDH-denominated Outcome Markets (binary prediction-style contracts) and the Zcash (ZEC) spot market going live on March 9. These moves enhance Hyperliquid's trading stack—adding prediction markets alongside its dominant perps and spot offerings—while tapping into renewed privacy coin momentum. HIP-4 positions Hyperliquid against platforms like Polymarket, with portfolio margin upgrades boosting capital efficiency for high-volume traders. The ZEC listing, limited to unshielded transactions, aligns with institutional Zcash infrastructure builds like Foundry's upcoming mining pool. ## HIP-4 Testnet: Outcome Markets and Portfolio Margin Upgrades

HIP-4 launched on testnet March 11, 2026 (02:01 UTC), debuting Outcome Markets as fully collateralized binary options based on HyperCore mark prices. Users access these via the "Predict" interface tab, with settlements in fixed price ranges, non-linear payouts, and expiration mechanics—no leverage or liquidations involved. Initial markets are periodic binaries; mainnet will start with 1-day expiries for BTC and HYPE, with multi-outcome support planned later. All markets use USDH collateral. [PANews](https://www.panewslab.com/en/articles/019cdaa0-abf2-72dd-819d-de34ae3f78ea)[Chaincatcher](https://www.chaincatcher.com/en/article/2251015)

Simultaneously, the next network upgrade transitions portfolio margin from pre-alpha to alpha, expanding from test accounts to mainnet portfolios under ~$500K. Eligibility requires weighted trading volume >$5M. Key USDH limit increases take effect post-upgrade:

| Limit Type           | Previous | New       | Impact                                                                                  |
| -------------------- | -------- | --------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Global Supply Cap    | N/A      | 500M USDH | Scales ecosystem borrowing                                                              |
| Global Lending Cap   | N/A      | 100M USDH | Boosts liquidity provision                                                              |
| Per-User Supply Cap  | N/A      | 5M USDH   | Enables larger positions                                                                |
| Per-User Lending Cap | N/A      | 1M USDH   | Improves efficiency for whales [BlockBeats](https://en.theblockbeats.news/flash/335507) |

This upgrade arrives as HIP-3 open interest hit a $1.26B ATH (up from $500M monthly prior), underscoring Hyperliquid's perp dominance. HIP-4 extends this into predictions, potentially capturing volume from offchain rivals amid $2-3B weekly prediction market activity. ## Zcash (ZEC) Spot Launch

Spot ZEC deposits, withdrawals, and trading activated March 9, 2026 (20:49 UTC), announced by integration partner Unit. Currently, only unshielded ZEC is supported—shielded (privacy-enhanced) deposits/withdrawals pending. This enables liquid, anonymous spot trading on Hyperliquid, combining ZEC's zk-SNARKs privacy with Hyperliquid's orderbook depth. Community reaction highlights it as a "massive win" for Zcash liquidity and industry anonymity. [X](https://x.com/unitxyz/status/2031110163533541801)[X](https://x.com/SneakyAlgo/status/2031132852155462142)

ZEC spot fits Hyperliquid's expansion into non-perp assets, following HIP-3's permissionless listings (e.g., gold, Nasdaq). Recent bridged ZEC DEX volume on Solana hit $17M weekly, signaling cross-chain demand Hyperliquid can now capture directly. [X](https://x.com/SolanaSensei/status/2029884960690294867)
