## TL;DR

As of March 18, 2026 (05:56 UTC), Hyperliquid has not added new core exchange perpetuals in the traditional sense over the last 7 days. Instead, the platform's growth stems from its HIP-3 permissionless perpetuals framework (live since October 2025) and a major integration with Byreal Perps (launched March 17, 2026). This has unlocked explosive activity in Real World Assets (RWAs), with Crude Oil (CL-USDC) leading amid Middle East tensions—its cumulative volume surged from $200M to $6B post late-February Iran strikes. HIP-3 open interest shattered records at $1.43B on March 14, representing 23% of total platform volume and capturing 70%+ of perp DEX market share. ## HIP-3: Permissionless RWA and TradFi Perps

HIP-3 enables anyone staking 500k HYPE (~$18.5M at $37) to deploy custom perps with 2x fees (50% to deployer, 50% to protocol for buybacks). No new HIP-3 "launches" were announced last week, but activity exploded in existing markets:

- Commodities: Crude Oil (CL-USDC) dominated with $1.2B 24h volume peaks; Gold/Silver followed.
- Equities/Indices: NVDA, CRCL tokenized stocks; S&P 500/Nasdaq futures.
- Pre-IPO: OpenAI (458 first-time users), SpaceX (727 first-time on-chain txns). [PANews](https://www.panewslab.com/en/articles/019cf9c1-9cfd-756c-a578-deec0bce9b8f)

12 of top 20 volume markets are HIP-3, with 90% OI from Trade.xyz (Hyperunit tokenization). Deployers handle oracles/limits; isolated margin only (cross coming soon).
