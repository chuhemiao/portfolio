## 💡 Core Idea

Export your Google Chrome bookmarks → Import them into YouMind → AI automatically analyzes and categorizes them → Generate your personal navigation site

Say goodbye to the saying "collecting means forgetting"!

---

## 🛠️ Detailed operating steps

### Step 1️⃣: Export Favorites

- Open the Chrome/Edge browser

- Bookmark Manager → Export Bookmarks as HTML File

### Step 2️⃣: Import into YouMind

- Upload the HTML file to YouMind

- All links are automatically converted into manageable content.

### Step 3️⃣: AI Intelligent Classification

- AI automatically analyzes link content

- Categorized by topic: Podcasts, Technology, AI Tools, Finance, Learning...

- Automatic detection of duplicate linksandBroken links🔥

### Step 4️⃣: Generate your personal navigation site

- Generate a visual navigation panel with one click.

- supportDark/Light Mode

- Minimalist black and white design, exuding a high-end feel.
