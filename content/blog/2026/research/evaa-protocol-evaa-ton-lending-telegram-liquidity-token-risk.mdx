## Mechanism And Value Capture

EVAA's core loop:

1. Users supply assets into lending markets.
2. Borrowers use collateral to borrow assets.
3. The protocol earns spreads or fees.
4. EVAA can capture value through governance, staking, incentives, revenue routing, or access.

The Telegram angle is useful because TON users can access DeFi inside familiar UX. But lending protocols are judged by risk controls, oracle design, collateral quality, liquidation execution, and bad-debt history.

## Competitive Landscape

EVAA competes with TON lending protocols, Aave-style money markets on larger chains, Telegram mini-app financial products, and centralized yield products. Its edge is TON-native and Telegram-native UX. Its weakness is smaller ecosystem liquidity and early token float.

## Red-team Check

The strongest bear case is that EVAA's TVL is real but token capture is weak, while lending risk remains high. The most gameable metric is TVL if incentives or recursive borrowing are involved. The zero path is collateral failure, bad debt, and token unlock pressure.
