## Economics and Value Capture

The positive case is that decentralized derivatives liquidity and perps infrastructure creates recurring activity and that SNX captures part of it through fees, staking, governance, access, burns, collateral, or incentives. The weak case is that the product can grow while SNX remains only a rewards or governance asset. For this reason, I would track usage and token sinks before treating SNX as more than watchlist exposure.

## Red-team Check

The strongest bear case is that Synthetix grows attention or integrations without creating durable demand for SNX. The most gameable metric is headline partnerships or volume without retained users. The token value-capture failure path is product usage that bypasses token sinks.
