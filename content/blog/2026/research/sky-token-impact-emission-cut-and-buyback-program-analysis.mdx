## Governance Changes: Emission Cut and Buyback Details

Executed post-March 5 vote (passed Feb 12, executed Feb 13), these align with Sky's scaling to $11B+ USDS supply:

- Emission Cut: Reduces staking emissions by 162M SKY over 180 days (~900K SKY/day). This slashes daily inflation from prior levels, directly benefiting holders by curbing supply growth. Staking rewards continue uninterrupted via existing reserves. [CoinDesk](https://www.coindesk.com/markets/2026/03/05/sky-jumps-nearly-10-after-governance-vote-cuts-emissions-while-buybacks-tighten-supply)
- Buyback Program: $114.5M total (~3.6M SKY/day at $0.078), historically funded by 75% of net profits (revenue minus expenses). This removes tokens programmatically, tightening circulating supply and creating buy pressure. Expansion of USDS lending further boosts protocol fees to sustain it.

These aren't one-offs: Sky's model prioritizes SKY destruction via buybacks (97% revenue historically looped back), differentiating it from pure governance tokens.

## Key Nuance: March 10 Temporary Adjustment

A new proposal (voting open until 2026-03-12 16:00 UTC) temporarily reallocates buybacks from 75% to 7.5% of net profits for ~3 months, redirecting to USDS backstop capital. This drops daily removal to ~360K SKY (90% cut), prioritizing protocol resilience as USDS scales.

- Why? Builds "stronger protection for USDS holders" amid rate adjustments (Sky Savings Rate to 3.75%). Reserves trackable on-chain. [X](https://x.com/SkyEcosystem/status/2031358144224383310)[X](https://x.com/SkyEcosystem/status/2031358150608077153)
- Token Effect: Mutes near-term boost (net removal falls ~90%), but staking rewards persist. Post-3 months, buybacks revert to 75%, amplifying later impact.

No passage risk evident—leading "Yes" with 6.8B SKY support.
