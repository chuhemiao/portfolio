## 2. Product & Technical Stack

Core modules:

- Tokenized pre-IPO assets (e.g., SPACEX mint: PreANxuXjsy2pvisWWMNB6YaJNzr7681wJJr2rHsfTh, FIGURE: PreAYsissANcRXg5uTniKqdCrrUSGdvtrKDVPHS1ib6)
- SPV-backed asset structure for economic mirroring
- Secondary trading via Solana DEXs (Jupiter limit orders launched March 20, 2026)
- Wallet integrations (OKX, Bitget, Backpack, Banana Gun, etc.)

System design: SPVs acquire shares or interests in private companies off-chain; Solana tokens represent proportional, non-voting economic exposure (price tracking), not equity ownership. Trading executes peer-to-peer on DEXs, with platform facilitating liquidity routing. Recent Jupiter integration enables limit orders for assets like OpenAI ($1,163) and SpaceX ($693), driving Day 1 volume to $9.37M across 6 tokens. [PreStocks](https://prestocks.com/products) [X](https://x.com/top7ico/status/2034954208495964497)

Key mechanics:

- Fractional ownership (no $1M+ minimums)
- 24/7 instant liquidity vs. TradFi lockups
- Redemption to USDC (partial, fee-based, no guarantee)

External integrations:

- Solana DEXs (Jupiter, Raydium, Titan, DFlow)
- DeFi wallets/tools (composability for lending/collateral unproven at scale)

The design prioritizes accessibility but relies on centralized SPV management, creating a hybrid CeFi-DeFi model.

## 5. Protocol Revenue & Economics

Revenue sources: Bid-ask spreads + DEX routing fees; tied to volume (e.g., $9.37M Day 1 implies meaningful capture).

Economic model: B2C marketplace; earns on trades regardless of direction. Platform MC $10M reflects speculative pricing, not AUM.

Key questions:

- Liquidity: Thin (~$1.5M platform-wide); reliant on Jupiter for depth.
- Prices: Speculative (e.g., SPACEX premium to implied val); private mkt opacity risks inefficiency.
- Sustainability: Volume-dependent; post-hype drop from $9M to $184K/SPACEX signals event-risk.

No TVL/revenue dashboards; model viable if listings expand but exposed to dry-up.
