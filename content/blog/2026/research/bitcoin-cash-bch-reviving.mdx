## 2. What BCH Is Today

BCH is a 2017 BTC hard fork emphasizing larger blocks (32MB) for payments. Current role: marginal Layer 1 with peer-to-peer cash branding (bch.info). Market rank ~#16 (CoinGecko), 0.6% BTC dominance equivalent. Fact: Trading ~1.5% up 24h but underperforms BTC (+3.9%). No TVL/fees data available (TokenTerminal gap); on-chain muted vs. 2017-2021 peaks.

## 3. Thesis For Owning BCH

- Low fees/utility niche: Median tx fee $0.0016 enables micro-payments (BitInfoCharts). OnePay (Walmart-linked) added BCH support (2026-03-29 news).
- Dev continuity: BCHN v29.0.0 (May 2026) activates 4 CHIPs for scripting/scalability (GitHub releases).
- Speculative re-rating: Halving cycle (next ~2028) + predictions ($600-1200/2026 per TradingView) if BTC rally spills over. Inference: Tactical dip-buy if BTC breaks $70k.

## 4. Thesis Against Owning BCH

- Declining relevance: Active addresses/tx dwarfed by LTC (70k/171k), TRON (high vol implied). BitPay 2025 stats: BTC/stablecoins dominate (40% stablecoin vol).
- Security vulnerability: Hash rate 6 EH/s (~0.6% BTC's); miner exodus risk post-halving (CoinWarz/BitInfo).
- No insti tailwinds: Grayscale BCHG exists but AUM/premium unknown (fetch failed); no ETF momentum vs. BTC/ETH filings (Morgan Stanley, etc.).
