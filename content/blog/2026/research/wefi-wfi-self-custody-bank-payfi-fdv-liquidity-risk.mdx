## Project Overview

WeFi describes itself as a payment system and self-custody bank account experience. Surf frames the product as a way for users to manage cryptocurrency and traditional currency through an accessible self-custody account. [WeFi](https://wefi.co/) [Surf](https://agents.asksurf.ai/)

The project spans BNB Chain, Ethereum, Arbitrum, Polygon, Linea, and zkSync Era, which helps distribution but increases contract and bridge-surface complexity.
