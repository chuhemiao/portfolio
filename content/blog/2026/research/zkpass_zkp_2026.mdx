## TL;DR

zkPass is a hybrid zkTLS oracle protocol that converts private Web2 data into on-chain, zero-knowledge proofs without exposing the underlying information. The mainnet and the $ZKP token went live in late 2025, supported by $18 M in venture funding and fresh CEX listings. With >250 K on-chain interactions, 353 K social followers, and an SDK that delivers millisecond proofs, zkPass is emerging as core privacy infrastructure for identity, reputation, and compliance use cases.

## 2. Protocol Architecture & Technical Stack

### 2.1 High-Level Flow

1. **3P-TLS Handshake**: Prover (P) and Verifier (V) jointly establish TLS session keys with Data Source (S).
2. **Traffic Recording**: V records ciphertext; P alone decrypts.
3. **Local Proof Generation**: P computes predicates over the plaintext and produces a VOLE-in-the-Head proof in milliseconds.
4. **On-chain / Off-chain Verification**: V (or a dApp) verifies the proof through zkPass verifier contracts or SDK helpers. [paper](https://paper.zkpass.org/tech.pdf)

### 2.2 Cryptographic Primitives

- **3P-TLS** (Elliptic-curve DH) + **MPC** (Paillier, OT) secure the session.
- **VOLE-ZK** & **SoftSpokenOT** yield lightweight, transparent proofs (no trusted setup).
- **ZKVM** wraps circuits for flexible statement logic. [paper](https://paper.zkpass.org/tech.pdf)

### 2.3 Trust & Security Assumptions

- Relies on standard TLS authenticity.
- Split MAC keys prevent prover forgery.
- No centralized servers; proofs are locally generated, minimizing data-custody risk. [zkPass](https://zkpass.org)

### 2.4 Developer Tooling

- **TransGate-JS-SDK** (npm `@zkpass/transgate-js-sdk`) for browser & Node integrations.
- Proof-as-a-Service endpoints, schema templates, tutorial repos, and a browser extension. [GitHub](https://github.com/zkPassOfficial)

### 2.5 Smart-Contract Interfaces

Example verifier contracts (EVM/Solana) are included in `zkPass-VOLEitH-Showcase`; token contract on Ethereum: `0xe1be...dbc`. [GitHub](https://github.com/zkPassOfficial)

## 6. Governance & Risk Analysis

1. **DAO Structure** – Swiss Association; Treasury 4-of-5 multisig; 7-day proposal/vote cycle. [zkPass](https://zkpass.org)
2. **Technical Risks**  
   • TLS downgrades or BGP hijacks could mis-route traffic (mitigated via MPC mode).  
   • Web2 dependency – server IP blocks may require dual-client fallback.  
   • Implementation bugs in VOLE-ZK circuits; ongoing audits pre-TGE. [paper](https://paper.zkpass.org/tech.pdf)
3. **Economic Risks** – Token utility tied to verification demand; low fee capture today.
4. **Oracle Collusion** – Slashing & BLS-aggregated randomness discourage validator mis-behavior.

## 7. Project Stage & Strategic Positioning

- **Current Maturity**: Early mainnet with live incentives but still polishing schemas & integrations.
- **Bottlenecks**: UX (browser extensions), developer education, regulatory clarity for KYC proofs.
- **Strategic Edge**: First-mover hybrid 3P-TLS + VOLE-ZK, enabling light client proofs without server changes; larger addressable market than price feeds.
- **Competitive Outlook**: Outperforms heavier MPC-TLS (TLSNotary, PADO) in latency; differentiated from Chainlink/DECO by transparent setup and flexible schemas.
