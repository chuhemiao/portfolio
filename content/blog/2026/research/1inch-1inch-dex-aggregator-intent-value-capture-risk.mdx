## Economics and Value Capture

The positive case is that DEX aggregator and intent/MEV routing protocol creates recurring activity and that 1INCH captures part of it through fees, staking, governance, access, burns, collateral, or incentives. The weak case is that the product can grow while 1INCH remains only a rewards or governance asset. For this reason, I would track usage and token sinks before treating 1INCH as more than watchlist exposure.

## Red-team Check

The strongest bear case is that 1inch grows attention or integrations without creating durable demand for 1INCH. The most gameable metric is headline partnerships or volume without retained users. The token value-capture failure path is product usage that bypasses token sinks. The plausible impairment path is liquidity decline, token unlock pressure, weak usage data, or a better-funded competitor taking category share.
