## Red-team Check

The strongest bear case is that APE remains a token from the 2021-2022 NFT cycle, with brand recognition but weak new user demand. The most gameable metric is ecosystem announcement count. The value-capture failure path is ApeChain activity growing while fees, gas, and app value do not accrue meaningfully to APE.
