## Pre-screen Decision

Full research. apyUSD is a newer yield-bearing stablecoin with meaningful market cap, RWA framing, and unusual collateral mechanics. It deserves full coverage because users could mistake the asset for a low-risk dollar token.

## TL;DR

apyUSD is a yield-bearing stablecoin inside the Apyx protocol. Apyx describes its model as dividend-backed digital credit rather than a traditional fiat-backed stablecoin ([Apyx](https://apyx.fi/), [Apyx Docs](https://docs.apyx.fi/)). Surf shows apyUSD around $1.013 on June 28, 2026, with about $123.1M market cap, $471.6K 24h volume, and about 121.5M circulating supply. The core thesis is not "stable dollar cash." It is exposure to a yield wrapper backed by off-chain dividend streams and Digital Asset Treasury preferred-share economics.

Bottom line: apyUSD is an RWA/yield watchlist asset, not reserve collateral. The key diligence item is whether collateral, redemption, and dividend cash-flow reporting are transparent enough to justify the yield premium.

## Project Overview

Apyx uses apxUSD as a synthetic dollar liquidity layer and apyUSD as the yield-bearing wrapper. The docs describe apxUSD as over-collateralized and dividend-backed, not a traditional fiat-backed stablecoin. Surf lists APYUSD on Ethereum with contract `0x38eeb52f0771140d10c4e9a9a72349a329fe8a6a`, and apxUSD with contract `0x98a878b1cd98131b271883b390f68d2c90674665`.

## Research Question

Can apyUSD deliver transparent yield from off-chain digital-credit income without introducing hidden collateral and redemption risk?

## Architecture and Mechanism

The simplified flow is: DAT preferred-share exposure produces dividend income; apxUSD acts as a synthetic dollar liquidity layer; apyUSD acts as a yield-bearing savings wrapper. This means the risk is not the same as USDC or T-bill-backed stablecoins. Users are exposed to asset quality, legal structure, dividend reliability, redemption mechanics, and oracle/accounting assumptions.

## Economics and Value Capture

apyUSD holders seek yield through a wrapper rather than price appreciation. The protocol captures value if it can source dividend-backed collateral, maintain trust, and scale liquidity. APYX governance may capture upside if protocol assets and adoption grow, but apyUSD itself should be evaluated as a risk-bearing savings asset.

## Red-team Check

The strongest bear case is that users price apyUSD like a stablecoin while the backing behaves like structured credit. The most gameable metric is headline APY if collateral risk is under-disclosed. The value-capture failure path is yield compression plus redemption friction. The zero path is collateral impairment, legal lockup, or loss of confidence in redemption.
