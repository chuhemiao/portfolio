## Economics and Value Capture

The positive case is that non-custodial wallet plus CeFi/DeFi access layer creates recurring activity and that SFP captures part of it through fees, staking, governance, access, burns, collateral, or incentives. The weak case is that the product can grow while SFP remains only a rewards or governance asset. For this reason, I would track usage and token sinks before treating SFP as more than watchlist exposure.

## Red-team Check

The strongest bear case is that SafePal grows attention or integrations without creating durable demand for SFP. The most gameable metric is headline partnerships or volume without retained users. The token value-capture failure path is product usage that bypasses token sinks. The plausible impairment path is liquidity decline, token unlock pressure, weak usage data, or a better-funded competitor taking category share.
