## Pre-screen Decision

Full research. Fartcoin is a large Solana meme token with major exchange listings and meaningful volume. It deserves coverage as a liquid cultural-beta asset, not because it has protocol fundamentals.

## TL;DR

Fartcoin is a Solana meme token launched through Pump.fun and represented by the official site [fart.dev](https://fart.dev/). Surf shows listings through Coinbase, Binance Alpha, and Bitget. As of June 28, 2026, FARTCOIN traded around $0.127 with about $127.0M market cap, fully diluted valuation roughly equal to market cap, and about $12.2M 24h volume. That makes it more liquid than many mid-cap infrastructure tokens.

Bottom line: Fartcoin is tactical meme exposure. The asset can outperform in a Solana attention cycle, but the investment case is not earnings, usage, or infrastructure. Position sizing should reflect reflexive downside.

## Research Question

Is Fartcoin a durable Solana meme benchmark, or is it a liquidity-rich attention trade whose value decays when the meme cycle rotates?

## Architecture and Mechanism

There is no complex protocol mechanism. Fartcoin is a Solana token whose primary mechanics are fixed-like supply, exchange liquidity, social propagation, and speculative attention. That simplicity is both the point and the risk: holders are underwriting culture and liquidity, not productive cash flow.

## Economics and Value Capture

Fartcoin has no direct revenue capture. Value comes from meme premium, exchange access, Solana ecosystem attention, and the willingness of new buyers to pay for cultural liquidity. There is no fee stream, no staking security requirement, and no protocol cash flow to defend a valuation.

## Team and Governance

Surf does not list a formal team or funding. For a meme token, that is normal, but it changes the diligence lens: holder concentration, contract controls, liquidity depth, exchange support, and social resilience matter more than a roadmap.

## Red-team Check

The strongest bull counterargument to skepticism is that meme coins do not need fundamentals if they own attention. The most gameable metric is social engagement. The value-capture failure path is simply that attention rotates. The zero path is delisting, liquidity collapse, and the meme becoming culturally stale.
