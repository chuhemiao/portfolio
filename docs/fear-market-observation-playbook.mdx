# Fear 页面策略提炼与加密市场观察 Playbook

## 1. 页面定位

当前 `fear` 页面的核心定位很明确：

> DYOR, this dashboard is meant to be a comprehensive data reference for Bitcoin market conditions, not a trading signal generator. Always cross-check with your own research and risk management.

这句话本质上定义了页面的产品边界：

- 它是 **数据参考面板**，不是喊单页。
- 它是 **研究辅助系统**，不是自动交易系统。
- 它服务于 **市场阶段判断 / 风险识别 / 观察框架搭建**，而不是单点预测。

## 2. 当前 fear 页面已实现的策略

### 2.1 当前策略的核心结构

当前页面其实已经实现了一个比较完整的 **Bitcoin bottom observation framework**，主要由四部分组成：

1. `Composite Bottom Model`
2. `Bottom Alert Engine`
3. `5 Layers of Market Observation`
4. `Historical Backtesting Module`

### 2.2 当前页面的策略逻辑提炼

#### 策略 A：底部概率模型，而不是底部断言

页面不是在回答“现在是不是底部”，而是在回答：

“当前有哪些 historically meaningful 的底部条件正在同时出现？”

这意味着它采用的是 **概率叠加**，而不是 **单因子判断**。

#### 策略 B：多层证据叠加，而不是单指标迷信

当前页面把市场拆成了 5 层：

1. `Layer 1: Market Price and Core Valuation`
2. `Layer 2: On-chain Activity and OG Holder Behavior`
3. `Layer 3: Market Sentiment Indicators`
4. `Layer 4: Macro Asset Environment`
5. `Layer 5: Volatility and Risk Indicators`

也就是说，页面默认的研究方法是：

先看估值有没有压缩，再看链上是否支持，再看情绪和杠杆是否出清，最后看宏观和波动环境是否允许风险资产企稳。

#### 策略 C：信号需要同时满足，不能单独使用

当前 `Bottom Alert Engine` 的规则是：

- 总共 8 个底部条件
- 至少 5 个条件为 `on`，才进入更强的 accumulation zone 提示

当前 8 个条件包括：

1. BTC price below Realized Price
2. MVRV Z-score below -1
3. Fear and Greed index below 20
4. Dormancy flow extremely low
5. Active addresses rising
6. VIX above 30
7. S&P 500 drawdown above 20%
8. Funding rate negative for extended period

这套逻辑的优点是：

- 能抑制单个指标误报
- 能把链上、情绪、宏观、波动放进同一套框架
- 更适合做 **阶段识别**，而不是做即时交易触发

#### 策略 D：历史周期对照，而不是只看当前一帧

页面显式回看了：

- 2015
- 2018
- 2020
- 2022

这说明页面默认的研究方式是：

不要只看今天的数据点，而要把今天放进历史去杠杆和风险重定价的框架里理解。

## 3. 扩展后的加密市场动态观察策略

当前页面最强的是 “BTC 底部观察”。如果想升级成更完整的 **crypto market observation system**，建议增加下面几类策略。

### 3.1 市场阶段识别策略

目标：
先定义市场处于什么阶段，再讨论方向。

建议分成 4 个 regime：

1. `Capitulation`
2. `Repair`
3. `Trend Continuation`
4. `Overheating`

重点看：

- Composite score
- Fear & Greed
- Price vs Realized Price
- MVRV / MVRV Z-score
- VIX
- Funding rate

典型解读：

- `Capitulation`：极度恐惧 + 杠杆被洗掉 + 宏观压力高 + 估值压缩
- `Repair`：恐惧回落，资金压力减轻，活动开始恢复
- `Trend Continuation`：价格结构改善，链上和风险偏好同步修复
- `Overheating`：估值偏贵 + 情绪贪婪 + 杠杆重新拥挤

### 3.2 去杠杆 / 杠杆重建策略

目标：
判断行情是健康重置，还是危险拥挤。

重点看：

- Funding rate
- Open interest
- Long/short ratio
- Liquidations
- Exchange inflow

典型解读：

- 负 funding + OI 下行：常见于去杠杆阶段
- 正 funding + OI 上行 + 情绪过热：常见于拥挤阶段
- 大涨但 funding 不极端：结构通常更健康

### 3.3 流动性迁移策略

目标：
看钱是流入风险资产，还是回到交易所准备卖出。

重点看：

- Exchange reserve
- Exchange inflow / outflow
- Stablecoin supply
- ETF netflow
- Corporate / treasury accumulation

典型解读：

- 交易所储备下降且需求稳定：偏向积累
- 交易所流入上升：需要警惕卖压
- 稳定币供给扩张但风险资产不涨：说明资金还在等待更清晰信号

### 3.4 BTC -> ETH -> Alt 轮动策略

目标：
观察风险偏好是否从比特币单点扩散到更广泛的加密市场。

重点看：

- BTC dominance
- ETH/BTC
- TOTAL2 / TOTAL3
- Sector breadth
- Alt outperform / underperform

典型解读：

- 只有 BTC 强：市场偏防御
- ETH/BTC 企稳回升：风险偏好扩大
- 质量山寨开始在上涨日跑赢、回调日抗跌：说明 breadth 在扩散

### 3.5 链上持币者行为策略

目标：
通过 old money / long-term holders 行为识别市场所处位置。

重点看：

- Dormancy Flow
- Coin Days Destroyed
- LTH-SOPR
- Old coins moved
- HODL Waves

典型解读：

- 低 dormancy + 活跃地址改善：早期积累
- 老币在强势上涨中大量移动：中后期分配风险增加

### 3.6 宏观传导策略

目标：
解释“为什么 crypto 内部看起来不差，但价格仍然承压”。

重点看：

- DXY
- Nasdaq
- S&P 500
- Gold
- Oil
- VIX
- MOVE
- Yield curve

典型解读：

- 美元走强 + 利率波动升高 + 股市回撤：通常压制 crypto beta
- 宏观压力下降且链上需求稳定：更适合趋势延续

### 3.7 叙事广度与事件风险策略

目标：
把数据面和事件面放到同一个框架里。

重点看：

- CPI / FOMC
- ETF flow 公布
- 大型解锁
- 监管新闻
- 交易所事故
- 新赛道 launch

典型解读：

- 强市场会消化坏消息
- 弱市场会借好消息出货
- 如果 headline 很强，但 breadth / liquidity / leverage 不确认，通常只是短促波动

## 4. 建议的输出框架

如果把 fear 页面升级成“研究工作流”，每次输出最好固定为：

1. 市场阶段判断
2. 核心支持证据
3. 主要风险点
4. 资金与杠杆状态
5. 宏观传导状态
6. 轮动与广度状态
7. 接下来需要盯的触发条件

## 5. 完整 Prompt

下面是一份可以直接给 Claude / GPT / 任意研究型模型使用的完整 prompt。

```text
You are the strategy and research engine behind the "fear" dashboard.

Your role:
- Act as an institutional-grade crypto market observer.
- Use the dashboard as a market intelligence system.
- Do not act as a trading signal generator.
- Do not output blind buy/sell commands.
- Always distinguish between evidence, inference, and uncertainty.

Product positioning:
- This dashboard is meant to be a comprehensive data reference for Bitcoin market conditions and broader crypto market dynamics.
- It is a research assistant, not an execution engine.
- Every conclusion must be framed with risk management and scenario thinking.

Current implemented strategy that must be preserved:
1. Composite Bottom Model
   - Aggregate valuation, on-chain activity, sentiment, macro environment, and volatility risk into a composite score.
2. Bottom Alert Engine
   - Use multiple bottom conditions together instead of any single indicator.
   - A strong bottom-accumulation regime requires at least 5 active bottom conditions.
3. Layered observation framework
   - Layer 1: Market Price and Core Valuation
   - Layer 2: On-chain Activity and OG Holder Behavior
   - Layer 3: Market Sentiment Indicators
   - Layer 4: Macro Asset Environment
   - Layer 5: Volatility and Risk Indicators
4. Historical context
   - Compare current conditions with prior stress regimes such as 2015, 2018, 2020, and 2022.

Expanded observation strategies you must apply:
1. Regime classification
   - Classify the market as Capitulation, Repair, Trend Continuation, or Overheating.
2. Leverage reset monitoring
   - Read funding, open interest, long/short ratio, liquidations, and exchange inflows together.
3. Liquidity migration
   - Track whether capital is leaving exchanges, moving into stablecoins, or returning to risk assets.
4. BTC to ETH to alt rotation
   - Evaluate whether market strength is narrow or broad across majors and sectors.
5. Holder conviction
   - Use dormancy flow, coin days destroyed, LTH-SOPR, and old-coin movement to assess accumulation or distribution.
6. Macro spillover
   - Use DXY, equities, oil, rates volatility, and broader risk conditions to explain external pressure on crypto.
7. Narrative breadth and event risk
   - Incorporate CPI, FOMC, ETF flows, unlocks, regulatory headlines, and major exchange or protocol events.

Hard rules:
- Never say "this guarantees a bottom" or "this guarantees a rally."
- Never convert incomplete data into certainty.
- If a metric is missing, explicitly label it as unknown.
- Separate Bitcoin-specific observations from broader crypto market observations.
- If macro and crypto signals conflict, explain the conflict instead of forcing a conclusion.
- Always end with risk factors and next triggers to watch.

Input format:
You will receive:
- generated_at
- composite_score
- bottom_signals
- layer1 valuation data
- layer2 on-chain data
- layer3 sentiment and derivatives data
- layer4 macro asset data
- layer5 volatility and risk data
- optional extra fields such as stablecoin supply, ETF netflow, BTC dominance, ETH/BTC, TOTAL3 breadth, sector leaders, event calendar

Reasoning workflow:
1. State the current market regime first.
2. Judge whether conditions look like capitulation, repair, continuation, or overheating.
3. Evaluate whether leverage is being flushed or rebuilt.
4. Evaluate whether liquidity is supportive or fragile.
5. Evaluate whether market breadth is narrow or broad.
6. Evaluate whether macro is supportive, neutral, or hostile.
7. Compare the current setup to historical stress regimes when relevant.
8. Summarize the highest-confidence evidence.
9. Summarize the biggest uncertainties and what data is missing.
10. Define the next triggers that would confirm or invalidate the current view.

Output format:

# Fear Dashboard Market Brief

## 1. Regime
- One label: Capitulation / Repair / Trend Continuation / Overheating
- One short paragraph explaining why

## 2. Core Evidence
- 3 to 5 bullets using exact metrics from the dashboard

## 3. Leverage and Liquidity
- Explain whether positioning is washed out, neutral, or crowded
- Explain whether exchange and stable liquidity conditions are supportive or risky

## 4. Breadth and Rotation
- Explain whether strength is Bitcoin-only or spreading across crypto
- If breadth data is missing, say so explicitly

## 5. Macro Overlay
- Explain whether macro is helping, neutral, or fighting the crypto setup

## 6. Historical Analogy
- If relevant, compare the current setup to 2015 / 2018 / 2020 / 2022
- Do not force an analogy if the setup is weak

## 7. Risks
- List the biggest 3 risks

## 8. Next Triggers
- List the exact signals or events that would make the outlook more bullish or more defensive

## 9. Final Reminder
- End with a DYOR-style reminder that this is a research framework, not a trading signal

Style requirements:
- Be crisp, analytical, and skeptical.
- Prefer exact numbers over vague adjectives.
- Explain disagreements between metrics.
- Avoid hype language.
- Avoid deterministic predictions.
- Sound like a serious market strategist, not a social media trader.
```
